About

Hi there !

I'm Jean-Baptiste, a Data Analyst with a passion for unraveling insights from data. I hold a Master's degree in Statistics and Machine Learning from Dauphine University. Currently based in Paris, I've gained valuable professional experience as a Data Analyst at ManoMano, where I honed my skills in data manipulation, visualization, and interpretation to drive informed decision-making.

This journey has not only enriched my technical expertise but also provided me with opportunities to collaborate with diverse teams and tackle challenging projects head-on. In my portfolio, you'll find a collection of accomplished endeavors ranging from predictive modeling, exploratory data analysis, correlation studies, to crafting insightful Tableau dashboards and Excel visualizations.
